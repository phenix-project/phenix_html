AI and Phenix
====================

Phenix is supplied with several AI tools that can help you decide what to
do and to help you solve your structure:

- The Phenix `chatbot <https://phenix-online.org/chatbot>`__ will
  answer your questions about Phenix and how to use Phenix tools and how
  to solve a structure

- The Phenix `AI agent <ai_agent.html>`__ can automatically analyze your
  X-ray or cryo-EM data and solve your structure. It can also run many
  Phenix tools for you.

- The Phenix `AI analysis tool <ai_analysis.html>`__ can look at you log
  file and suggest what to do next.

If you are a beginner, ask the 
`chatbot <https://phenix-online.org/chatbot>`__
how to solve your structure if you have X-ray data (or cryo-EM data). Use
the `AI agent <ai_agent.html>`__ to automatically set up and analyze your
data for you,and use the `AI analysis tool <ai_analysis.html>`__ 
and the `chatbot <https://phenix-online.org/chatbot>`__ to interpret your
results.

If you are an advanced user, try the `AI agent <ai_agent.html>`__ as a tool
for quickly screening your data.

If you are either a beginner or an advanced user,
also have a look at the overall
Phenix `documentation <https://phenix-online.org/documentation/>`__
for tips and ideas for solving your structure.
